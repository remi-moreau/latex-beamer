Here are some basic expressions in quantum mechanics:

- State: \( \ket{\psi} \)
- Inner product: \( \braket{\phi}{\psi} \)
- Expected value: \( \expval{A} = \matrixel{\psi}{A}{\psi} \)
- Projection operator: \( \ketbra{\psi}{\phi} \)
